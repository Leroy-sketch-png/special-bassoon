# Financial Assistance Scheme (FAS) — Policy Summary

## What is FAS?
The Financial Assistance Scheme (FAS) is an MOE programme that provides financial support to Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents who face genuine financial difficulties in meeting education-related expenses.

## Eligibility Criteria
- Applicant must be a Singapore Citizen or Permanent Resident enrolled in an MOE-registered institution.
- Gross monthly household income must be at or below SGD $3,000, OR per capita income at or below SGD $750 per month.
- Applicant must demonstrate genuine financial hardship.

## What FAS Covers
- Subsidies on tuition fees.
- Assistance with miscellaneous fees.
- Some schemes extend to school uniforms, textbooks, and enrichment programmes depending on the tier approved.

## Application Process
1. Begin an FAS application in the e-Service portal.
2. An AI assistant will guide you through collecting required information.
3. Review the draft application — fields auto-filled from government data are read-only.
4. Confirm the information is accurate, tick the consent and declaration boxes, and submit.
5. Applications are processed within 10 working days of full submission.

## Required Application Fields
- **household_income**: Monthly gross household income in SGD (must be provided by applicant).
- **household_size**: Total number of persons in the household (must be provided by applicant).
- **num_dependants**: Number of dependants the applicant supports (must be provided by applicant).
- **school_name**: Name of the educational institution (must be provided by applicant).
- **year_of_study**: Current year or level of study (must be provided by applicant).
- **reason_for_application**: Brief description of financial circumstances (must be provided by applicant).
- **nationality**: Automatically verified from government records — read-only.
- **consent_given**: Must be manually ticked by the applicant. Cannot be auto-filled.
- **declaration_true**: Applicant declares all information is true and correct. Must be manually ticked. Cannot be auto-filled.

## Important Notes
- The AI assistant will NOT ask for your NRIC or Singpass identity — these are already verified.
- The AI assistant will NEVER auto-tick consent or declaration checkboxes on your behalf.
- Only submit if all information is accurate — false declarations may result in disqualification and legal action.
