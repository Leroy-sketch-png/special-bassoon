# Education Account Lifecycle Rules

## Account Creation
- Education Accounts are automatically created when a Singapore Citizen turns 16 years of age.
- The account is created on or after the citizen's 16th birthday.
- Only Singapore Citizens are eligible. Permanent Residents and foreigners are not eligible for automatic account creation.

## Account Closure
- Education Accounts are automatically closed when a citizen turns 30 years of age.
- Education Accounts are immediately closed upon the confirmed death of the account holder.
- Administrative officers with HQ_ADMIN role may manually override account status with a recorded reason.
- Manual closure reasons include: death, fraud, administrative correction.

## Account Balance
- Balances may be used to offset eligible education-related invoices issued by MOE-registered institutions.
- The Education Account balance is deducted first (up to the invoice total). Any remaining amount must be paid via an external payment method (PayNow or card).
- Unused balances at account closure are subject to MOE's dormant account policy; they do not automatically refund.

## Split Payment Rules & Portal Steps
- When paying an invoice or course fee via the e-Service portal, follow these steps:
  1. Log in to the MOE e-Service portal using Singpass.
  2. Navigate to the "Bills" or "Invoices" section on your dashboard.
  3. Click on the outstanding bill or course fee you wish to pay.
  4. The system will calculate how much of the invoice can be covered by your Education Account balance.
  5. Click the "Pay Now" button.
  6. If the balance is greater than or equal to the invoice total, the full amount is deducted automatically.
  7. If the balance is less than the invoice total, you will be redirected to HitPay to pay the remaining balance via PayNow or credit card.
  8. If the balance is zero, the full invoice total must be paid via HitPay.

## Invoice Statuses
- PENDING: Invoice issued, payment not yet made.
- PARTIALLY_PAID: Some payment received but not yet full.
- PAID: Full payment confirmed.
- CANCELLED: Invoice cancelled by MOE.
